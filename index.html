Why Free Tiers Surprise You

Silent Expiration

Azure's 12-month free services don't stop when they expire—they just start charging you. AWS monthly limits just reset and keep going.

Hidden Dependencies

A "free" VM triggers billable disks, IPs, and bandwidth. AWS Lambda needs S3, CloudWatch, and more. One service becomes five charges.

No Hard Stops

Both clouds let you exceed limits and bill you later. No warnings, no budget locks by default. You find out when the invoice arrives.

Everything You Need

🎯 Free Tier Tracking

Monitor Azure (65+ services) and AWS (100+ services) against free tier limits. Real-time visibility into what you're using.

⚠️ Smart Alerts

Set budget alerts for Azure ($1-$100) and usage thresholds for AWS (e.g., 80% of EC2 hours). Get warned before charges hit.

📊 Cost Breakdown

See current month spending by service. Forecast trends. Understand exactly where your money is going across both clouds.

🧹 Cleanup Guide

Interactive guide to find and remove unused resources. Stay within limits without manual hunting through consoles.
